Who We Are

GDR CONSTRUCTION COMPANY is a premier civil construction organization with over 30 years of rich experience in handling prestigious civil contracts across Rajasthan and pan-India. The company was founded by highly experienced engineers who have previously held directorial positions in top-tier construction firms in Jaipur.

Our foremost objective is to handle large-scale projects with the highest quality of construction and time-bound completion of work. To achieve this, the organization deploys highly qualified, competent staff and sufficient Plant & Equipment.

Every structure erected by GDR Construction is the manifestation of the vision carried by our directors and staff — to give each client utmost quality and peace of mind while working with us.

We specialize in Civil Structural Concrete Construction Works, right from the foundation stage to completion, including form-work, steelwork, concreting, and all finishing works.
