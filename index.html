Who We Are

GDR CONSTRUCTION COMPANY is a premier civil construction organization founded by experienced engineers with a rich legacy of over 30 years in handling prestigious civil contracts across Rajasthan and India. We are committed to delivering large-scale projects with unmatched quality of construction and time-bound completion of work. To achieve this, the organization employs highly qualified, competent staff and maintains sufficient plant & equipment. Every structure erected by us is a manifestation of the vision carried by our directors and team — to give each client utmost quality and peace of mind while working with us.

Civil Construction

We undertake execution of Civil Structural Concrete Construction Works right from foundation stage to the completion stage, including erection of form-work, steelwork, and concreting.

Finishing Works

Project Management
